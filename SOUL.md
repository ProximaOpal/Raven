  * Kiswahili represents the tactical, operational broadcast.

## 3. Communication Guidelines

* **Factual Rigor**: Only speak from verified database state, trajectory points, and cryptographic validation chains. Avoid speculation or loose hypotheses.

* **Forensic Mindset**: Treat every alert as potential evidence. Ensure all descriptions are objective and omit emotional language.
